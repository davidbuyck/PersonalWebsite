About David R. Buyck, PhD(c)

Innovator and expert in medical simulation, extended reality (XR), and AI technologies, dedicated to transforming healthcare training and patient outcomes worldwide.

Professional Bio

With a robust academic background in Computer Science and Bioinformatics & Computational Biology, David Buyck has spent years innovating at the intersection of technology and healthcare. As Founder and CEO of AstrumArc LLC and the Lead Software Engineer at the University of Minnesota’s Visible Heart Laboratories, David architects cutting-edge XR, AI-powered simulations and educational tools used by healthcare professionals globally.

His deep technical expertise in XR, artificial intelligence, software development, and cloud infrastructure uniquely positions him to tackle complex challenges in medical training. Currently a PhD candidate focusing on accelerating medical education, he combines rigorous research with real-world application to improve patient outcomes worldwide.
