The arc

I’m driven by complex systems, high-stakes workflows, and tools that must perform when reality is messy.

I build interactive software where correctness, latency, and usability all matter at the same time. In medicine, that means turning complex anatomy, device behavior, and procedural logic into systems that can teach, guide, and scale.
